## 🏢 Who We Are

**TUINNOV8 SOLUTIONS LTD** is a premier software engineering firm and institutional innovation accelerator headquartered in **Kenya**. We operate at the intersection of robust enterprise software development, intelligent automation, and startup incubation — designing, deploying, and scaling tailored digital solutions across:

| Pillar | Focus |
|:------:|:------|
| 🏢 **Commercial Enterprises** | Driving efficiency, scalability & digital transformation for businesses of all sizes |
| 🏛️ **Government Sectors** | Building secure, scalable public-sector infrastructure & digital service delivery |
| 🌍 **NGOs** | Crafting high-impact, data-driven platforms for development & community empowerment |
| 🚀 **Startup Acceleration** | Nurturing early-stage startups with technical scaffolding & strategic mentorship |

> 💡 **Core Backbone — E-Health Solutions:** Our fundamental pillar is designing comprehensive E-Health solutions engineered to democratize health accessibility across East Africa.

## 💚 Open Source at TUINNOV8

<div align="center">

**We believe in the power of open source to drive innovation forward.**

</div>

While most of our software is **proprietary licensed** (see [LICENSE.md](./LICENSE.md)), we actively encourage and support open source development within our community. Repositories that are open source will be **clearly marked** with the appropriate license in their respective `LICENSE` file.

> 🌱 _We believe that sharing knowledge and code accelerates innovation for everyone. If you see a repository without an explicit open-source license, it falls under our default proprietary terms._

### 🏛️ Multi-Organization Structure

TUINNOV8 operates across **several GitHub organization profiles** depending on the scope, domain, or partnership context of a project. This allows us to:

- 🎯 **Isolate project scopes** for cleaner access control and CI/CD pipelines
- 🤝 **Facilitate partnerships** with dedicated shared spaces for collaborators
- 📦 **Modularize ecosystems** — each org focuses on its core domain

> Look for the **TUINNOV8** branding across our affiliated organizations to identify our projects.

### 📌 Quick Contribution Rules

- ✅ All PRs must include comprehensive **unit & integration tests**
- ✅ Follow **semantic commit messages** (e.g., `feat(ehealth): add patient portal API`)
- ✅ Branch from `develop` using prefixes: `feature/`, `fix/`, `hotfix/`, `refactor/`
- ✅ **Zero-Leakage Policy** — Never commit secrets, API keys, or credentials
- ✅ At least **one approved review** from a Senior Engineer before merge
